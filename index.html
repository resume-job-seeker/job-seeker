About This Site

This is an independent research and writing resource. Content covers workforce development, employment documentation, career transitions, and hiring systems — written from a practitioner and policy analysis perspective. Articles draw on publicly available government data, regulatory guidance, and labour market research.

Topics covered include writing strong ATS-friendly resumes, improving job applications, career transitions, interview preparation, reentry employment, outplacement services, and adult skills funding compliance.
